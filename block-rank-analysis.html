Block-Rank Analizi v2 — dispute/refund split + create-weighted score

Hansı 5-dim combo-ları blokladıqda ən az gəlir itirib ən çox dispute/refund qarşısı alınar. Create disputes 1.5× weight (CAC).

Bu analiz nəyi göstərir

Hər ödəniş-müştəri kombinasiyası üçün — yəni (subscription tipi × ölkə × ştat × kart şəbəkəsi × bank) — biz iki şeyi müqayisə edirik: o combo-dan əldə etdiyimiz uğurlu gəlir və o combo-da yaranmış itki (bank chargeback + refund). Məqsəd: hansı combo-ları gateway-də bloklasaq, ən az legitimate gəlir itirib ən çox dispute/refund qarşısı alarıq?

Loss iki növə bölünür çünki onların biznes təsiri fərqlidir: bank chargeback (dispute) Stripe-da chargeback ratio-nu artırır və fee-ə səbəb olur (4 ay ardıcıl >0.7% = restriction); refund (prevention service-lər, chargeback911, admin manual) fee yaratmır amma gəliri itiririk. Bu səbəbdən hər combo üçün ayrıca dispute share göstərilir — eyni 20% loss rate-ə malik iki combo fərqli aksiya tələb edə bilər.

Score-da create dispute-ları 1.5× ağırlıqla dəyərləndirilir, çünki yeni müştəri cəlb etmək üçün marketing CAC sərf edirik — bir create-dispute = revenue itki + CAC itki. Renew isə artıq cəlb edilmiş müştəridən "costsuz" gələn gəlirdir, ona görə renew bloklamaq nisbətən daha bahalıdır.

Statistik mənaverən nəticələr üçün hər combo minimum tx_count ≥ 30 AND revenue ≥ €3,000 AND loss > 0 şərtini ödəməlidir; bu, kiçik təsadüfi outlier-ləri (məs. 1 tx 1 dispute = 100% rate) çıxarır.

SQL — Platform total stats query 
SELECT
    t.description AS sub_type,
    if(c.type = 'dispute', 'dispute', 'refund') AS loss_type,
    round(SUM(c.amount * c.exchange_rate / 100), 2) AS loss_eur,
    count(*) AS cnt
FROM qrcode_data.chargebacks c FINAL
JOIN qrcode_data.transactions t FINAL ON t.id = c.transaction_id
WHERE t.description IN ('Subscription create','Subscription renew')
  AND NOT (c.type = 'dispute' AND c.status = 'won')
  AND NOT (c.type IN ('refund','partial_refund') AND c.status = 'failed')
GROUP BY sub_type, loss_type;

-- Revenue:
SELECT t.description, count(*) AS tx,
       round(SUM(t.amount * t.exchange_rate / 100), 2) AS rev_eur
FROM qrcode_data.transactions t FINAL
WHERE t.status = 'succeeded'
  AND t.description IN ('Subscription create','Subscription renew')
GROUP BY t.description;

SQL — Combined ranking query (weighted_score DESC) 
SELECT
    sub_type, country, state_norm AS state, brand, issuer_raw AS issuer,
    countIf(event_type = 'REVENUE') AS tx_count,
    countIf(event_type = 'DISP')    AS disp_count,
    countIf(event_type = 'REFUND')  AS refund_count,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE'), 2)                   AS revenue_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'DISP'),    2)                   AS disp_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REFUND'),  2)                   AS refund_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND')), 2)          AS loss_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND'))
          / sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE') * 100, 2)           AS loss_rate_pct,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'DISP')
          / sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE') * 100, 2)           AS disp_rate_pct,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REFUND')
          / sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE') * 100, 2)           AS refund_rate_pct,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'DISP')
          / nullIf(sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND')), 0) * 100, 2)
                                                                   AS combo_disp_share_pct,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND'))
          / sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE') * 100
          * if(any(sub_type) = 'Subscription create', 1.5, 1.0), 2) AS weighted_score
FROM (
    SELECT 'REVENUE' AS event_type, t.description AS sub_type,
           a.country, if(a.country IN ('US','CA'), a.state, NULL) AS state_norm,
           pm.card_brand AS brand, pm.issuer AS issuer_raw,
           t.amount * t.exchange_rate / 100 AS amt
    FROM qrcode_data.transactions t FINAL
    JOIN qrcode_data.payment_methods pm FINAL ON pm.id = t.payment_method_id
    JOIN qrcode_data.accounts a FINAL ON a.id = t.account_id
    WHERE t.status = 'succeeded'
      AND t.description IN ('Subscription create','Subscription renew')

    UNION ALL

    SELECT if(c.type = 'dispute', 'DISP', 'REFUND'),
           t.description, a.country,
           if(a.country IN ('US','CA'), a.state, NULL),
           pm.card_brand, pm.issuer, c.amount * c.exchange_rate / 100
    FROM qrcode_data.chargebacks c FINAL
    JOIN qrcode_data.transactions t FINAL ON t.id = c.transaction_id
    JOIN qrcode_data.payment_methods pm FINAL ON pm.id = t.payment_method_id
    JOIN qrcode_data.accounts a FINAL ON a.id = t.account_id
    WHERE t.description IN ('Subscription create','Subscription renew')
      AND NOT (c.type = 'dispute' AND c.status = 'won')
      AND NOT (c.type IN ('refund','partial_refund') AND c.status = 'failed')
)
GROUP BY sub_type, country, state_norm, brand, issuer_raw
HAVING tx_count >= 30 AND revenue_eur >= 3000 AND loss_eur > 0
ORDER BY weighted_score DESC LIMIT 40;

SQL — CREATE-only deep-dive query 
SELECT
    country, state_norm AS state, brand, issuer_raw AS issuer,
    countIf(event_type = 'REVENUE') AS tx_count,
    countIf(event_type = 'DISP')    AS disp_count,
    countIf(event_type = 'REFUND')  AS refund_count,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE'), 2)                   AS revenue_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'DISP'),    2)                   AS disp_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REFUND'),  2)                   AS refund_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND')), 2)          AS loss_eur,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND'))
          / sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE') * 100, 2)           AS loss_rate_pct,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'DISP')
          / sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE') * 100, 2)           AS disp_rate_pct,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'DISP')
          / nullIf(sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND')), 0) * 100, 2)
                                                                   AS combo_disp_share_pct,
    round(sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE') * 100 / 27025796.0, 4) AS revenue_pct_of_create,
    round((sumIf(amt, event_type IN ('DISP','REFUND')) - sumIf(amt, event_type = 'REVENUE'))
          * 100 / 27025796.0, 4)                                    AS net_loss_pct_of_create
FROM (
    SELECT 'REVENUE' AS event_type,
           a.country, if(a.country IN ('US','CA'), a.state, NULL) AS state_norm,
           pm.card_brand AS brand, pm.issuer AS issuer_raw,
           t.amount * t.exchange_rate / 100 AS amt
    FROM qrcode_data.transactions t FINAL
    JOIN qrcode_data.payment_methods pm FINAL ON pm.id = t.payment_method_id
    JOIN qrcode_data.accounts a FINAL ON a.id = t.account_id
    WHERE t.status = 'succeeded' AND t.description = 'Subscription create'

    UNION ALL

    SELECT if(c.type = 'dispute', 'DISP', 'REFUND'),
           a.country, if(a.country IN ('US','CA'), a.state, NULL),
           pm.card_brand, pm.issuer, c.amount * c.exchange_rate / 100
    FROM qrcode_data.chargebacks c FINAL
    JOIN qrcode_data.transactions t FINAL ON t.id = c.transaction_id
    JOIN qrcode_data.payment_methods pm FINAL ON pm.id = t.payment_method_id
    JOIN qrcode_data.accounts a FINAL ON a.id = t.account_id
    WHERE t.description = 'Subscription create'
      AND NOT (c.type = 'dispute' AND c.status = 'won')
      AND NOT (c.type IN ('refund','partial_refund') AND c.status = 'failed')
)
GROUP BY country, state_norm, brand, issuer_raw
HAVING tx_count >= 30 AND revenue_eur >= 3000 AND loss_eur > 0
ORDER BY loss_rate_pct DESC LIMIT 30;

Əsas tapıntılar

  • BR Nubank create tək başına €20K loss — 99.6% refund pattern (chargeback911/prevention triggerlər), bank dispute yox. Tövsiyə: friction artır (CVV strict, AVS strict, 3DS challenge) — block etmə çünki €71K revenue.
  • BR Itaú create €3.5K loss, 100% refund, 0.076% revenue payı — eyni pattern, refund-heavy. Bütün BR banklarında oxşar.
  • US Discover ştat-larda (WI, IN, IL) bank-dispute heavy (70-79% disp share). Tövsiyə: BLOCK + mandatory 3DS — chargeback ratio təhdidi.
  • US Capital One ştat-larda mixed (37-50% disp). 8 fərqli combo top 30-da (MA, MT, NY, UT, NC). Issuer-wide 3DS pressure tətbiq etmək olar.
  • Citi Costco (NC, AZ, MI, WI, TX) 5 combo top 30-da, 14-37% disp share. Costco-spesifik anti-fraud pattern var.
  • Renew-larda dispute rate yüksəkdir (8.74% vs 4.85%) AMMA renew bloklamaq daha bahalıdır (free money). Renew watchlist üçün 3DS challenge ilə kifayətlən.
  • FR/CH/DE/BE/IT premium banklar <1% rate — fast-lane (3DS exempt) imkanı var, conversion artırılması mümkündür.
